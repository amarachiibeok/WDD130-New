Overview

Purpose

Trendy fashion is a fashion label with over 6 years of experience in clothe production, shoes retailing, hair importation and delivery to individuals and fashion homes. It has served over 2000 clients within the last 3 months from twelve major cities in Africa, with commendable level of client satisfaction. It is proud of how far it has come in this line of business. This website is to help potential clients access "trendy clothings" goods and services in 3 core areas of business; quality and affordable clothes, shoes, and natural/synthetic hair, including delivery services to any part of Africa, looking forward to impressing new customers with high-quality and affordable products, and quick delivery services.

Audience

Male and female from twelve major cities in Africa, who are old enough to fend for themselves and have the capacity to use the internet and also have access to computer, desktop or laptop to access and preorder products easily . Who are seeking for high-quality services and are looking for an affordable and quality clothing and good natural/synthetic hair that will last for the longest time, with good delivery services within a short periodof time, at the comfort of their homes
