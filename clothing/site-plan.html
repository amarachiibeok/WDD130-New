Overview

Purpose

Trendy fashion is a fashion label with over 6 years of experience in clothe production, shoes retailing, bags and delivery to individuals and fashion homes. It has served over 2000 clients within the last 3 months from twelve major cities in Africa, with commendable level of client satisfaction. It is proud of how far it has come in this line of business. This website is to help potential clients access "trendy clothings" goods and services in 3 core areas of business; quality and affordable clothes, shoes, and bags, including delivery services to any part of Africa, looking forward to impressing new customers with high-quality and affordable products, and quick delivery services.

Audience

Male and female from 3 major cities in Africa, who are old enough to fend for themselves and have the capacity to use the internet and also have access to computer, desktop or laptop to access and preorder products easily . Who are seeking for high-quality services and are looking for an affordable and quality clothing and good natural/synthetic hair that will last for the longest time, with good delivery services within a short periodof time, at the comfort of their homes

Our offer

Trendy clothing: Trendy clothing’s make use of high-quality materials and fabrics that have the potential to withstand the environmental conditions peculiar with west Africa to create products that will suit the need of our clients. Our products range from cultural, social, and corporate wears for adult, children, males, and females. We make products with techniques and methodology that considers and respects environmental sustainability. Some of our products are made from recycled materials which guarantees the quality, and many other raw materials such as Synthetic materials, synthetic fibres, fabrics, Cotton, Cellulosic fibers/viscose, wool, Silk, Leather, Bast fibres, and experimental fabrics. We have products on display, and we also make customized products depending on our clients need. Trendy shoes: Our shoes and foot wares are high quality products made from different materials such as leathers, textiles, synthetics, rubber, foam, and plastic, animal skin, fabrics, recycled foil, cotton, “Africa bata” etc. These products are carefully designed to guarantee safety, comfort, style, class, healthy living, and beauty. We make products for various occasions such as wedding, cocktails, cultural ceremonies, fashion contest, industrial use etc. Our products are well designed for different ages and classes of people. With trendy shoes, just be sure you will find what you want. We also make customized products based on individual and group requirement. We deliver to your doorstep even before you know it. We are also available for retail distribution. Trendy bags: Our trendy bags come in different types, out of different materials such as Mesh Materials for Versatile and Sturdy, Leather Material for easy to Style, Nylon for flexible and affordable, Canvas for perfect choice for daily use, vegan leather an environment friendly material and Straw Material for a perfect picnic companion. We make for individual and group specification. We deliver at your doorstep and are open for retailers and wholesalers.
